THE PRINCIPLES OF BIOMETRICS


AJ Kumta

WHAT IS BIOMETRICS?


Biometrics is the measurement of unique physical characteristics for use within a device.

EXAMPLES OF BIOMETRICS:

SIRI


Uses voice recognition as a physical characteristic. Not used for authentication but still a biometric platform.

FACE RECOGNITION:

Uses a camera (or in some cases, lazers) to judge the structure of one's face.

TANGIBLE IDENTIFICATION: (IE, Fingerprint)

Where physical touch is a the base for unique identification of a person, by a device.
